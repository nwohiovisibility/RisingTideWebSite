Ways to Support

Rising Tide is powered by community members who believe in visibility, belonging, and expanding society’s understanding of normal. Your support helps us host events, create outreach materials, and build a stronger, more connected community.

Supporting Rising Tide also supports the Northwest Ohio Visibility Alliance (NOVA), our regional initiative focused on visibility and community empowerment.

Volunteer

Volunteers are the heart of Rising Tide. Whether you enjoy organizing events, helping with outreach, designing materials, or simply showing up to support others, there’s a place for you.

Donate

Donations help us cover event supplies, printing, outreach materials, and community support efforts. Every contribution — big or small — helps the tide rise for everyone.
